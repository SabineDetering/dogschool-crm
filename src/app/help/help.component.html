Getting started

1. Add clients

Go to the clients page and click on the + button to add a new client.

2. Add dogs

Go to the dogs page and click on the + button to add a new dog.

3. Enter trainings data

Go to the trainings page and click on the + button to add a scheduled training. You can complete the details on location, topics, exercises and homework later.

4. Dashboard

The dashboard gives you an overview of some topics that might need your attention.

  • "Upcoming trainings" shows you the scheduled trainings of the next 7 days so that you can prepare your training.
  • "Call to make an appointment" lists dogs that might need a new training appointment. Call the owner to stay in contact and schedule a training.
  • "Clients with incomplete data" and "Dogs with incomplete data" remind you to find out about missing information and complete your documentation.

    Editing

    All data shown in tables can be viewed in detail and edited by clicking on the corresponding table row.

    Settings

    Do you prefer dark mode? Then switch the slider to the moon in the upper right corner or on the settings page.

    Customise the selectable subjects of your trainings on the settings page. You can change, add or delete subjects or change their order by drag & drop.
